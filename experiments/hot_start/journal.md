# hot-start — make the seam a training view

## Pre-registration (2026-07-06, before any number exists)

integration-v1 priced the wall: the seam tax (mid-flight entry states
halve every specialist's stage rate; the artifact relay reads 13.3 %,
the clone 33.0 % at n=100). The clone anomaly named the mechanism —
oracle-BC repertoires are entry-state robust because the TEACHER was.
This campaign applies the twice-measured closed-loop law a third time,
at the integration layer: **collect where you will be examined.**

**Design (machinery in `scripts/distill.py`, selftested):**
- New teacher `OracleTrackW` — the width-gated tracker (holds when the
  aperture is too narrow AND the plane is near; lines up while
  waiting). **Teacher floor:** ≥ 0.80 on BOTH door@1.0 and odoor@1.0
  (n=30 each, seeds 121000+) or it does not enter the pot.
- `OracleRelay` — per-stage oracles over a composite course (Weave for
  gap/slalom, TrackW for the three movers), privileged and labelled.
- **The TRUE ceiling (probe 2):** the oracle relay on the STANDARD
  integration seeds (110000+, n=30). Frozen: below 0.55 the arena
  itself is the binding constraint and the campaign closes at the
  probe (the slalom-v2 pricing discipline).
- **Hot-start collection:** the relay flies 300 random courses (seeds
  120000+, disjoint from the 110000 exam series); the student
  observation is recorded with EXAM-EXACT StageLocal semantics
  (stage-local x, memory reset at entry); labels are relay actions;
  the seam states are real by construction. Pooled BC; manipulation
  floors: pooled val ≥ 0.80 AND per-stage-type val ≥ 0.80.
- **Exam:** the integration suite on the standard seeds — n=30 first;
  frozen graduation: ≥ 0.50 advances to the n=100 formal suite
  (baseline of record: clone 33/100). **Deployment gate unchanged:
  ≥ 0.70 at n=100 → videos of record.**

**Frozen signature.** Support = n=30 ≥ 0.50 (hot-start buys real seam
robustness). GREEN = n=100 ≥ 0.70 — the deployment gate opens and the
repo gets its first videos of record. Refuted = ≤ the clone's 0.33
band — seam robustness is not data-buyable at this altitude, and the
residue escalates (recurrent students / seam-specific curricula),
fresh pre-registration.
